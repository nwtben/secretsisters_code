Across the Obelisk
Here are some tools for Across the Obelisk, an amazing roguelike deckbuilder.
  • Obeliskial Options, a mod for changing gameplay, duplicating characters and enabling custom content;
  • Seed Checker, for ensuring you’re not wasting your time before starting a Madness 16 run;
  • Card/Item Creator, for use with Obeliskial Options;
  • Stop it, Sylvie!, a mod that replaces Sylvie’s hit sound;
  • Backseating Begone, a mod that disables receipt of some emotes;
  • Death’s Reach Returns, a mod that reverts audio changes to the Death’s Reach card;
  • Superlative Sandbox, a mod that increases Sandbox Mode limits;
  • Deck Distributor, a mod that enables import/export of decks at the Magic Forge; and
  • a fork of DontTouchFranky’s MoreTranslations, updated for Sands of Ulminin and the Japanese language.
