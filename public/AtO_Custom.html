The Basics
  • Install Obeliskial Options.
  • The easiest way to use the custom content creator is to  add a new card and use a vanilla card as the template.
  • When you are happy with your card (and its upgrades, which can be viewed from the  card list),  download it  and extract it to Across the Obelisk\BepInEx\config.
  • Ensure that Custom Content is enabled in the Obeliskial Options settings.
Multiplayer
You probably need to all have the exact same custom content. For now, I recommend zipping your entire "Obeliskial_importing" folder and sharing that :).
